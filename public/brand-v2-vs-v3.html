What to look for as you scan

Three things separate the two — and only one of them is subjective.

  • Objectively better in v3: gold contrast on cream lifts from 2.0 : 1 to 3.4 : 1. This is a legal / accessibility win, not a taste one.
  • Property‑aligned in v3: the cream matches actual room walls, walnut #8E5F30 mirrors the wood tone in 5 of 6 sampled rooms, and espresso becomes the deep card / dark‑panel surface. Chocolate + foil gold is the classic warm‑luxury pairing — Rosewood, Ralph Lauren Home, Bulgari, Bottega. Navy has been dropped from the primary palette; it's a photo tone the brand acknowledges but doesn't lead with.
  • Feel difference (taste call): Cormorant Garamond upright sits between Poppins startup and Fraunces couture — the classic Aman / Four Seasons / Le Bristol serif. Editorial without being operatic. Italic is reserved for one‑line poetic accents only.
